❓ Frequently Asked Questions
Everything you need to know before you start
Do I need to buy any expensive hardware? ▼
No! SmartPOS works on any phone, tablet, or laptop you already own. Just open it in Chrome. If you want thermal bill printing, you can buy a 58mm/80mm USB or Bluetooth thermal printer on Amazon for ₹1,500-3,000 — that's the only optional hardware.
What if my internet goes down? ▼
It still works! All plans include offline mode. Bills, orders, and menu changes are saved on your device. When your internet comes back, everything auto-syncs to the cloud. You never lose a bill.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime? ▼
Yes! Upgrade anytime — new features activate instantly. Downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle. No penalty, no lock-in. If you paid yearly, we'll adjust the remaining balance.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes — 7 days free on all plans! No credit card needed. Full features during trial. If you don't love it, you owe nothing. We'll even set it up for you and add your menu during the trial.
How does the 6-month and yearly billing work? ▼
Pay upfront and save 10% (6 months) or 20% (yearly). For example, Gold yearly = ₹9,600 (instead of ₹11,988 monthly) — you save ₹2,400! No surprises, no hidden fees, cancel anytime.
My staff isn't tech-savvy. Is it easy to use? ▼
Super easy! It's designed for waiters and cashiers, not tech people. Tap table → add items → settle bill. That's it. 5-minute training. Plus on Gold & Emerald, we come to your business and train your staff in person.
Can I use it on both my phone and laptop? ▼
Yes! Silver and above support multiple devices with real-time sync. Your cashier uses the laptop, you monitor from your phone, the kitchen sees orders on a tablet — all synced live via Firebase.
What's the difference between Gold and Emerald? ▼
Gold has everything a single-location business needs — KDS, delivery, payroll, AI assistant. Emerald adds multi-branch management, white-label branding, custom domain, WhatsApp/SMS notifications, loyalty programs, membership cards, hourly billing (snooker/gaming), wallet system, tournament management, API access, and dedicated priority support with on-site training.
Is my business data safe? ▼
100%. All data is stored in Google Firebase (same security as Google uses). Encrypted in transit and at rest. Only your authorized users can access it. You can export everything anytime as backup. Google Sheets auto-backup available on Silver+.
I run a snooker club — does it handle hourly billing? ▼
Yes! Emerald plan includes auto-start/stop timers for each table, hourly billing with different rates per table type, pause/resume, and a live wall display showing all table statuses. Perfect for snooker clubs, pool halls, and gaming lounges.
Can I try before I buy? ▼
Absolutely! Just call or WhatsApp us. We'll set up your POS with your menu, your tables, and your branding — all within 24 hours. Use it free for 7 days. No obligation. If you like it, choose your plan and continue. If not, no worries!
